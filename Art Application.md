Chapter 1: Defining Art

**What is Art?**
- is a creative activity that expresses ==imaginative or technical skills==. It is derived from a Latin term "*ars*" which means ability, skill, or craft.
- created by an artist based on his imaginations, thoughts, or experiences and it maybe from ==inspiration== of natural beauty.

**Traditional Art**
- "do it yourself art" originally practiced ==to serve a useful purpose==
**Liberal Art**
- art in a sense of a learned ==skill==

Three Classical Branches of Art
1. Painting
2. Sculpture
3. Architecture
